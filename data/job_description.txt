Key responsibilities include:
- Analyze financial data and develop forecasts, variance analysis, and budgets
- Assist in preparing monthly, quarterly, and annual financial reports
- Conduct cost analysis and profitability assessments
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to evaluate business performance
- Maintain dashboards and present KPIs to senior management

Required skills:
- Strong knowledge of Excel and financial modeling techniques
- Experience with budgeting, forecasting, and variance analysis
- Proficiency in data analysis tools such as Power BI or Tableau
- Familiarity with accounting principles (GAAP/IFRS)
- Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
- Strong written and verbal communication

Preferred:
- Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, Economics, or related field
- Experience with ERP systems like SAP or Oracle
- CFA Level 1 or MBA in Finance is a plus
